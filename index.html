5.000 randomly generated, unique, animated, flipping characters on the Ethereum blockchain.

Every Flipper is made out of various pieces.

Each piece comes in lots of different shapes and colors.

Every Mood Flipper comes with a uniquely generated sound design.

There are mixed in rarer traits, like Glasses, Earrings, Eyescolor, Metal pieces and more.

The collection also contains some hand made 1/1 Mood Flippers.

MoodFlippers are part of the MoodVerse. Designed and animated by Lucas Zanotto, @lucas_zanotto.
